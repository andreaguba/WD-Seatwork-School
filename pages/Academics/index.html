Education

Colegio De San Juan De Letran
offers elementary to collegiate education.

The Elementary Department of Colegio de San Juan de Letran has been serving young learners for nearly 403 years, offering education from Kindergarten through Grade School. Now part of the Basic Education Department, it includes Kindergarten followed by six years of elementary education.

The High School Department of Colegio de San Juan de Letran, which has existed for almost four hundred years now, has established its name as one of the most well-known secondary schools for boys in Manila.

The Senior Highschool Department offers courses for young adolescents fro m four different strands that comprises of each of its specialty

The Collegiate Department offers many courses that are open for students.
